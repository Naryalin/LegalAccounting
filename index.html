Trust Accounting

We handle trust account transfers, prepare necessary reports, reconcile monthly statements, and manage bank and documentation, keeping your trust accounts accurate and compliant.

Legal Accounting

We maintain invoice readiness by reconciling reimbursable costs, trust deposits, and client payments daily. Trust transfers are processed immediately upon payment clearance, ensuring your matters are always billing-ready.

Business Accounting

Our efficient methods provide real-time financial updates for bank and credit card accounts. Monthly reconciliations become a seamless result of daily processes, saving time and reducing complexity.

Cash Flow and Risk Management

Optimize cash flow to maintain liquidity for day-to-day operations. We identify cost-saving opportunities, streamline processes, and assess financial risks, ensuring your firm stays efficient and secure.

Financial Reporting and Analysis

Gain a comprehensive understanding of your firm's financial performance with accurate profit and loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports. Our insights help guide data-driven decisions and ensure transparency and compliance.

Budgeting and Strategic Planning

Prepare for the future with precise budgeting and forecasting. We provide tailored financial strategies to optimize resources, track performance, and adapt to market changes, supporting your firm's growth and resilience.

Optimization and Troubleshooting

Our optimization services address slowness and enhance PCLaw performance while resolving program errors and fixing data corruption. We also integrate PCLaw with tools like Outlook and Word, streamlining your workflows and improving efficiency.

Customization and Maintenance

We create electronic billing templates compatible with systems along with customized billing and document templates tailored to your firm's needs. Our backup services ensure your data is safe, with restoration solutions ready when needed.

Setup and Installation

We provide PCLaw installation and reinstallation services, ensuring smooth first-time setups or resolving corrupted software issues. Our team handles data migration between PCLaw versions and database conversions, maintaining data integrity and fixing any conversion-related errors.

FAQ

Your Legal Accounting Questions, Answered

Contact Us

How can I get started?

To get started, simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. Our team will guide you through the process.

What services do you offer?

We offer advanced bookkeeping, customized financial reporting on productivity and profitability, and ensure compliance with Law Society requirements, including trust account reconciliation and audit preparation. Our PCLaw expertise includes implementation, training, optimization, and support to streamline your firm's financial processes.

What makes us different?

What makes us unique is our specialized focus on the legal profession. At L4 Legal Accounting, we combine years of expertise in legal financial management with a deep understanding of Law Society compliance requirements. Our tailored approach ensures your firm’s bookkeeping, reporting, and financial needs are handled efficiently and accurately, allowing you to focus on delivering exceptional legal services while we take care of your finances.

Can you assist with Law Society compliance and spot audits?

Absolutely. We ensure that your financial records meet all Law Society requirements and provide support for trust bank reconciliations, annual reports, and spot audit preparation.

What credentials and experience do our advisors have?

Our advisors are highly qualified professionals with extensive expertise in the legal financial industry, holding advanced degrees and certifications. Led by our founder, Luis Garzon, CPA, CMA, a Chartered Professional Accountant and PCLaw Advanced Certified specialist, our team brings years of experience in legal accounting. We stay current with industry best practices to provide tailored bookkeeping, reporting, and financial management solutions, ensuring compliance with Law Society requirements and optimizing your firm’s financial processes.

Do you offer on-site or remote services?

We provide both on-site and off-site services, giving you the flexibility to choose what works best for your firm.

What types of clients do we work with?

We work exclusively with legal professionals, including sole practitioners, small partnerships, and medium-sized law firms.
